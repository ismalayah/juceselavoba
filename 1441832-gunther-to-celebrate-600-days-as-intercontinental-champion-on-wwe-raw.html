Gunther To Celebrate 600 Days As Intercontinental Champion On WWE RAW

Gunther will celebrate on the February 5 episode of WWE RAW.

WWE announced that Gunther will celebrate surpassing 600 days as WWE Intercontinental Champion on Monday night. Gunther won the gold on June 10, 2022, and he has since broken the record for the longest reign with the title. He most recently defended the gold against Kofi Kingston on the January 29 episode of WWE RAW.

The updated card is as follows:

  • Gunther celebrates 600 days as WWE Intercontinental Champion
  • : Kabuki Warriors (Asuka & Kairi Sane) vs. Kayden Carter & Katana Chance

WrestleZone will have full coverage of WWE RAW as it airs on Monday.
