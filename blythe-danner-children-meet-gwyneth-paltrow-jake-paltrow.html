Blythe Danner Children: Meet Gwyneth Paltrow, Jake Paltrow

Blythe Danner was married to film director Bruce Paltrow and together they share two children; Gwyneth Paltrow and Jake Paltrow.

Gwyneth Paltrow

Gwyneth Kate Paltrow is an American actress.

She was born on September 27, 1972 in Los Angeles, California, United States to actress Blythe Danner and film director Bruce Paltrow.

Gwyneth attended Crossroads School, before enrolling in the Spence School, an all-girls private school in Manhattan. Later, she studied art history at the University of California, Santa Barbara, before dropping out to act.

She is known for Shakespeare in Love (1998), Sliding Doors (1998), Iron Man 3 (2013), Emma (1996) and Who’s in My Bathroom? with Hailey Rhode Bieber (2022).

Gwyneth is the recipient of various accolades, including an Academy Award, a Golden Globe Award, and a Primetime Emmy Award.

She has been married to writer and producer, Brad Falchuk since September 2018.

Gwyneth Paltrow has two children with her former husband, English musician Chris Martin; Apple Martin and Moses Martin.

Jake Paltrow

Jake Paltrow is a director and writer.

He was born on September 26, 1975 in Los Angeles, California, United States to Bruce Paltrow and Blythe Danner.

Jake is known for Young Ones (2014), The Good Night (2007) and June Zero (2022).
Jake Paltrow is an American film director, screenwriter and actor.

He has been married to photographer and artist Taryn Simon since 2010 and together they share two children; Whister Paltrow and Eliel Paltrow.
