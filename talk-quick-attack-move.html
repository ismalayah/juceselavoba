Some Pokémon have the move twice in their moveset. If you caught a Pidgeot (using the Missingno. trick, for example) in Red and Blue, and it was level 6, it would know Quick Attack. If you DELETED Quick Attack for a TM move, it would try again to learn Quick Attack at level 12. Glad you asked, though. TTEchidna 07:33, 28 November 2008 (UTC)
