Eight-Year-Old Nakeeyat Claims She Has Never Been Paid For Her Services Because Of Her Age

Young talented poetic, Nakeeyat Dramani Sam has revealed that they don’t pay her after performing at Stakeholders shows.

According to her people sees her as a kid hence the reason stakeholders don’t pay for her services.

Speaking in an interview on Happy FM’s ‘Ayekoo after drive’ show she explained that she wish they pay her after the services but they don’t because they think she’s a child.

The Eight-year-old kid however added that she trains and rehearse for hours before doing any work.

Nakeeyat answered a question after she was asked if she tells them to pay her but she said they have to give it to her without asking.

” They have to do it themselves, I can’t ask them to pay, if I get the pay I will take it but they don’t I take the situation as it is” she noted.
