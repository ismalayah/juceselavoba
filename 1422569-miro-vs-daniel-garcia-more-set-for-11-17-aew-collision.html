Miro vs. Daniel Garcia, More Set For 11/17 AEW Collision

Three matches have been announced for the November 17 AEW Collision.

First, Miro take on Daniel Garcia in their first singles match as “The Redeemer” continues his dominant streak in the wake of CJ Perry aligning with Andrade El Idolo.

Elsewhere on the show, Dax Harwood will go one-on-one with RUSH of LFI just 24 hours before the two meet in a four-way AEW World Tag Team title match at Full Gear. This will mark their first singles match together.

Plus, AEW TBS Champion Kris Statlander will team up with AEW Women’s World Champion Hikaru Shida to take on Saraya and Ruby Soho of The Outcasts prior to their respective title defenses on Saturday.

Below is the updated lineup for November 17:

  • Dax Harwood vs. RUSH
  • Hikaru Shida & Kris Statlander vs. Saraya & Ruby Soho
  • Miro vs. Daniel Garcia

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of Collision as it airs during its special time of 8 p.m. ET on Friday.
